Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Overview

ClawControl is an open-source client application that connects to your self-hosted OpenClaw server. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data the app collects

ClawControl is a client that connects directly to your OpenClaw server. We do not operate any intermediary servers.

  • No telemetry. The app does not send analytics, crash reports, or usage data to us or any third party.
  • No account required. There is no ClawControl account. Authentication is between you and your OpenClaw server.
  • Local storage only. Settings (server URL, theme preference, sidebar state) are stored locally on your device via localStorage or the platform keychain. They are never transmitted to us.
  • Chat data stays on your server. All messages, sessions, and agent data are stored on and retrieved from your OpenClaw server. ClawControl does not store, cache, or forward chat content to any external service.

Data this website collects

The clawcontrol.app website is a static site hosted on AWS CloudFront/S3. We collect minimal data:

  • Beta signup form. If you sign up for the mobile beta, we collect your email address and platform preference (iOS/Android) via Google Forms. This data is used solely to notify you when the beta is available and is not shared with third parties.
  • Server logs. AWS CloudFront may log standard access information (IP address, requested URL, user agent, timestamp) for operational purposes. These logs are not used for tracking and are automatically deleted.

Third-party services

  • Google Fonts. This website loads fonts from Google Fonts, which may log your IP address per Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Forms. Beta signups are processed through Google Forms, subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub. Download links point to GitHub Releases. Downloading is subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Your rights

You may request deletion of any data we hold about you (e.g., beta signup email) by contacting us. Since the app itself collects no data, there is nothing to delete on the app side.

Children's privacy

ClawControl is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue on GitHub.
